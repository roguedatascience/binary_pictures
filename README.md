# Creating "binary" pictures

The orgininal intent of this project was to create a tool that performed a pretty simple task: convert images to black and white pixels and convert to black pixels into binary: 0s and 1s. But then the question arose: why stop at 0s and 1s and at black and white. This quickly ballooned into a pretty extensive image editing tool/app built in R shiny, that has very varied functionality, all centered around converting the pixels of images into graphical elements.

Tou can find the live app here: https://roguedatascience.shinyapps.io/binary_images/

To better understand the functionality of the tool, it's super helpdul to look at the following examples. You can find the high definition images as well as the app settings needed to produce them here: [El Pibe Valderrama example](pibe_example).
